## Optional ad-blocking filter subscriptions

ZZZ can download and locally cache the following filter lists when their subscriptions are enabled. The lists are not embedded in the application binary and remain the work of their respective maintainers.

- [EasyList](https://easylist-downloads.adblockplus.org/easylist.txt), [EasyList China](https://easylist-downloads.adblockplus.org/easylistchina.txt), [EasyPrivacy](https://easylist-downloads.adblockplus.org/easyprivacy.txt), and [Adblock Warning Removal List](https://easylist-downloads.adblockplus.org/antiadblockfilters.txt). EasyList's licensing terms offer GPL-3.0-or-later or CC BY-SA 3.0-or-later; see the [EasyList licence page](https://easylist.to/pages/licence.html).
- [CJX's Annoyance List](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/cjx82630/cjxlist/master/cjx-annoyance.txt), distributed under [LGPL-3.0](https://github.com/cjx82630/cjxlist/blob/master/LICENSE).

Updating a subscription replaces the local cached copy while preserving the list's original header and license metadata.
